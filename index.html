A new kind of family gathering.

Family Room is a multi-functional iOS application driven by the goal of connecting people with those they care about most. Users can interact with each other via group chat, media sharing, and by participating in phone and video conference calls.

Social Media-Style Group Chat

Family Room enables users to interact with posts, create new posts, and share media through its linear group chat. Each post is its own contained conversation, so only those who interact with the post will be notified upon further comments. The days of countless irrelevant notifications are over!

Organized Group Media

“Want to see adorable photos of my nephew?” says every aunt and uncle, ever. Family Room’s media feature allows users to specify exactly which type of media they want to see from the group’s collective album as well as who sent it. No more sifting through the group’s media history just to find that one baby photo!

In-app Conference Calling

Do you miss your family members constantly speaking over one another? No need to navigate into another app - Family Room offers in-app phone and video conference calling. Just place a call to connect with whoever is currently online. Seriously, it’s like you’re all in the same room.

Keeping in touch has never been this easy.
